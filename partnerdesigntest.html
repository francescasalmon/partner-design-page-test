Technical Reference

Display Unit (Defigo)

10.1" touchscreen (500 nits) made from UV-protected Makrolon plastic. Built-in 1080p wide-angle camera (130°), IP44 rating, and integrated RFID reader. Powered and connected via PoE+ directly from the Relay Hub. Operating temperature -20 °C to +50 °C.

Relay Hub (Control Unit)

The brain of the native system. Placed in a secure zone. Connects via 4G/LTE or wired LAN (CAT6). Powered by 110-230V. Equipped with 3 relay outputs (12V/24V/Potential-free) for up to 3 door environments, plus 1 input.

Smart Reader (Suprema Xpass2)

IP65/IP67 and IK08 rated. Powered via PoE or 12V/24V DC. Features offline support and built-in door relay. Available in Slim (Mullion), Standard (Gangbox), and PIN versions. Communicates directly with the cloud via standard Ethernet switches.

Secure I/O Module

Used for high-security perimeter doors with Smart Readers (Suprema). Connects to the reader's RS-485 input and is placed in a secure zone to prevent tampering with the door strike from the outside. Requires a separate power supply.
